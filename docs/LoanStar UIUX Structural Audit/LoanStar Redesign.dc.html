Assumptions & reasoning
Working from your structural audit (GitHub not connected yet). Context: Philippine seafarer-loan ops — internal staff tools + a borrower portal, so "warm & approachable" but still dense enough for daily operators. Sample data is invented but plausible (₱, aging buckets, pipeline stages from the audit). B and C use a neutral warm style so the IA reads independently of the A choice.
