Inline banner for approvals, due dates, missing documents, scheduled visits.

```jsx
<Alert variant="success" title="Application Approved">
  Your loan of ₱150,000 has been approved. Releasing is scheduled for June 18, 2026.
</Alert>
```

Variants: `success`, `warning`, `error`, `info` — always pair the color with a short title and one line of explanatory body text.
